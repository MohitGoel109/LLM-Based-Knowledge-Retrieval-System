Placement Cell - Campus Recruitment Guidelines
Academic Year 2024-25

1. Eligibility Criteria
   - Minimum CGPA of 6.0 (no active backlogs at the time of placement).
   - Students with backlogs cleared before the placement drive begins are eligible.
   - Only final-year and pre-final-year students may participate.

2. Registration Process
   - Register on the Placement Cell portal by August 15th each year.
   - Upload your updated resume (PDF format, max 2 pages).
   - Submit a passport-sized photograph and copies of all semester marksheets.
   - A one-time placement registration fee of Rs. 500 is applicable.

3. Placement Drive Rules
   - Students must attend the pre-placement talk (PPT) for any company they wish to apply to.
   - Dress code: Formal attire is mandatory during interviews and PPTs.
   - Once a student accepts an offer, they are removed from subsequent placement drives (One Offer Policy).
   - Students who receive a package above Rs. 10 LPA may opt to sit for "dream company" drives for packages above Rs. 15 LPA.

4. Internship Policy
   - Pre-final year students are encouraged to pursue summer internships (May-July).
   - Internship offers through the placement cell also follow the One Offer Policy.
   - Students must submit an internship completion certificate to the placement coordinator.

6. Companies Visited (2023-24)
   - TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Accenture
   - Amazon, Microsoft, Google (dream category)
   - Paytm, Razorpay, Zomato (startup category)
   - Average package: Rs. 6.5 LPA
   - Highest package: Rs. 42 LPA (Google)
   - Total students placed: 340 out of 420 eligible (81% placement rate)
