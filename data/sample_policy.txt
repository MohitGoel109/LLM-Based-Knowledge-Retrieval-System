College of Engineering - Internal Policy Document
Year: 2024

1. Attendance Policy
Students are required to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in all subjects. 
Failure to meet this requirement will result in debarment from the end-semester examinations.
Medical leaves must be submitted within 3 days of returning to campus.

2. Grading System
The college follows a 10-point CGPA system.
S Grade: 90-100 (10 points)
A Grade: 80-89 (9 points)
B Grade: 70-79 (8 points)
C Grade: 60-69 (7 points)
D Grade: 50-59 (6 points)
F Grade: Below 50 (Failure)

3. Library Rules
Books can be borrowed for a maximum of 14 days.
Late fine is Rs. 5 per day.
Silence must be maintained at all times.

4. IT Lab Guidelines
No food or drink allowed inside the labs.
Students must log off after their session.
Use of college network for non-academic purposes is strictly prohibited.
