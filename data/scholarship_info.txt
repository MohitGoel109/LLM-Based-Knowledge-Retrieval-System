Scholarship Information Guide
Academic Year 2024-25

1. Merit-Based Scholarships

   a) Dean's List Scholarship
      - Eligibility: CGPA 9.0 or above (no backlogs)
      - Benefit: 100% tuition fee waiver for the next semester
      - Selection: Automatic (based on semester results)

   b) Academic Excellence Award
      - Eligibility: CGPA 8.5â€“8.99, top 10% of the branch
      - Benefit: 50% tuition fee waiver
      - Selection: Automatic

   c) Branch Topper Award
      - Eligibility: Highest CGPA in each branch each semester
      - Benefit: Rs. 25,000 cash prize + certificate
      - Selection: Automatic

2. Need-Based Financial Aid

   a) Income-Based Fee Concession
      - Eligibility: Family annual income below Rs. 4,00,000
      - Benefit: Up to 75% fee waiver (based on income slab)
      - Required documents: Income certificate, BPL card (if applicable), Aadhaar card
      - Application: Submit to Dean of Student Affairs before September 30th

   b) Emergency Financial Assistance
      - For students facing sudden financial hardship (medical emergency, job loss of parent, etc.)
      - Benefit: One-time grant of up to Rs. 50,000
      - Application: Submit a request letter with supporting documents to the Student Welfare Office

3. Government Scholarships (External)

   a) Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students
      - Apply via National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in)
      - Deadline: Usually October 31st each year

   b) Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship
      - For students from families with income below Rs. 8,00,000
      - Benefit: Rs. 10,000/year for UG, Rs. 20,000/year for PG
      - Apply online at scholarships.gov.in

   c) State Government Merit Scholarship
      - Varies by state; check your state's education department website
      - Typically requires 12th board marks above 85%

4. Sports Scholarship
   - Eligibility: Represented at state or national level in any recognized sport
   - Benefit: Up to 25% tuition waiver + free hostel accommodation
   - Apply at the Sports Department with certificates and achievements

5. Research Fellowship (PG/PhD)
   - Eligibility: M.Tech/PhD students with GATE qualification
   - Benefit: Monthly stipend of Rs. 12,400 (M.Tech) or Rs. 31,000 (PhD)
   - Duration: 2 years (M.Tech), 5 years (PhD)

6. How to Apply
   - Visit the Scholarship Office (Block A, Room 102) or college website
   - Email: scholarships@college.edu
   - Phone: 011-2345-6785
   - Keep scanned copies of all documents ready (marksheets, income proof, ID, bank details)

Note: Students receiving multiple scholarships may be asked to choose one, as per college policy.
Multiple external government scholarships can be held simultaneously unless restricted.
