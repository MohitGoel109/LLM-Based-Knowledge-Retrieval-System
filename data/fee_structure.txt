Fee Structure - Academic Year 2024-25
College of Engineering

1. Tuition Fees
   - B.Tech (All branches): Rs. 1,25,000 per semester
   - M.Tech: Rs. 80,000 per semester
   - MBA: Rs. 1,50,000 per semester
   - Payment deadline: Within 30 days of semester commencement.
   - Late payment fine: Rs. 100 per day after the deadline.

2. Examination Fees
   - End-semester exam: Rs. 2,000 per semester (included in tuition)
   - Re-examination (backlog/KT paper): Rs. 500 per subject
   - Supplementary exam: Rs. 1,000 per subject
   - Improvement exam: Rs. 750 per subject

3. Hostel Fees (per semester)
   - Double sharing room: Rs. 10,000
   - Single room: Rs. 15,000
   - Mess charges: Rs. 27,000 (Rs. 4,500/month x 6 months)
   - Hostel caution deposit (refundable): Rs. 5,000

4. Other Charges
   - Library fee: Rs. 2,000 per year
   - Laboratory fee: Rs. 3,000 per semester
   - Sports and cultural fee: Rs. 1,500 per year
   - Student welfare fund: Rs. 500 per semester
   - Identity card: Rs. 200 (first issue free, Rs. 200 for replacement)
   - Convocation fee: Rs. 2,500 (charged in final semester)

5. Scholarships and Fee Waivers
   - Merit scholarship: Top 5% of each branch get 50% tuition waiver.
   - SC/ST/OBC students: Fee concession as per government norms.
   - Sports quota: Up to 25% fee waiver for national-level athletes.
   - Financial hardship: Apply to the Dean of Student Affairs with income certificate.
   - Application deadline: September 30th each year.

6. Payment Methods
   - Online payment via college portal (UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Card)
   - Demand Draft in favor of "College of Engineering" payable at local branch
   - EMI option available for tuition fees (max 3 installments per semester)

7. Refund Policy
   - Full refund (minus Rs. 1,000 processing fee) if withdrawal within 15 days of admission.
   - 50% refund if withdrawal within 30 days.
   - No refund after 30 days.
   - Hostel caution deposit refunded at the time of leaving hostel.
