Hostel Rules and Regulations
Effective from: January 2024

1. Hostel Timings
   - Entry Gate closes at 9:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 PM on weekends.
   - Late entry requires written permission from the Hostel Warden.
   - Night out pass must be applied for 24 hours in advance via the hostel portal.
   - A maximum of 4 night-out passes are allowed per month.

2. Room Allocation
   - Rooms are allotted on a first-come-first-served basis during admission.
   - Room changes are only allowed at the start of each semester with warden approval.
   - Each room accommodates 2 students (double sharing) or 1 student (single, subject to availability).
   - Single room charges: Rs. 15,000/semester. Double sharing: Rs. 10,000/semester.

3. Mess and Food
   - Mess timings:
     • Breakfast: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
     • Lunch: 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM
     • Snacks: 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM
     • Dinner: 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM
   - Mess fee: Rs. 4,500 per month (auto-deducted from hostel account).
   - Special diet requests (Jain, vegan) must be submitted to the mess committee.
   - Outside food delivery (Swiggy/Zomato) is allowed only in the common area.

4. Visitor Policy
   - Visitors are allowed between 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM only.
   - Parents can visit on any day; friends require prior visitor pass from the warden.
   - Opposite gender visitors are NOT allowed inside hostel rooms.
   - All visitors must register at the hostel reception with a valid ID.

5. Prohibited Items and Activities
   - Alcohol, tobacco, and narcotic substances are strictly banned.
   - Electric cooking appliances (induction, heater) are not allowed in rooms.
   - Playing loud music after 10:00 PM is prohibited.
   - Gambling in any form is strictly prohibited.
   - Violation of any of the above results in a fine of Rs. 5,000 and possible expulsion from the hostel.

6. Maintenance and Complaints
   - Submit maintenance requests via the Hostel App or at the reception desk.
   - Electrical and plumbing issues: resolved within 24 hours.
   - Furniture replacement requests: processed within 1 week.
   - Emergency contact (24/7): Security Office - 011-9876-5432

   - Girls Hostel Warden: Dr. Sunita Rao (sunita.rao@college.edu)
